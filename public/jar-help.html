🔧 Troubleshoot Transaction

Follow these steps to resolve your pending or failed withdrawal quickly.

1
Check your bank account

Credits can take 2–4 hours. Check bank passbook or SMS alerts before raising a ticket.

2
Verify your UPI ID is active

Go to Profile → Bank Details and confirm your linked UPI ID matches your bank name.

3
Check withdrawal limits

Daily limit ₹50,000 · Monthly limit ₹2,00,000. Exceeding limits will hold the withdrawal.

4
Wait for TAT (up to 48 hrs)

If still pending after 48 hours, contact our support team directly below.

💡 Tip: Keep your registered mobile number and transaction ID ready for faster resolution.
